About me

Hello, My name is Anna Colberg, and I'm currently in the middle of my apprenticeship in web development. As part of an intensive bootcamp, I'm learning the fundamentals of modern web technologies such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, as well as Git and GitHub. What I particularly enjoy about this field is the ability to combine creativity with technology and develop solutions. I'm curious, quick to learn, and determined – exploring new content independently or quickly familiarizing myself with complex topics is one of my strengths. In addition to my professional development, I place great value on teamwork and collaboration. I enjoy working with others, sharing knowledge, and growing together on projects. Openness, reliability, and respectful interaction are a given for me. My goal is to continuously expand my skills, develop professional web solutions, and work in an environment that encourages innovation, creativity, and team spirit.
